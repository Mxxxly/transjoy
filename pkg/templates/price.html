How We Calculate Your Price

Factors Influencing Your Final Delivery Cost

Our dynamic pricing model ensures you only pay for what you need. The final cost of your JoyXpress delivery is primarily based on three key factors:

  • Distance & Route Optimization
    The direct mileage between pickup and drop-off points.
  • Weight & Size (Dimensional Weight)
    Heavier/larger items may require different vehicle types.
  • Delivery Speed (Standard vs. Express)
    Choosing express service incurs a small priority surcharge.
